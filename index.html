Predicting Titanic Passenger Survival

In the "Predicting Titanic Passenger Survival" project, I explored the fascinating world of data science and machine learning. By analyzing historical data from the ill-fated Titanic voyage, I developed predictive models to determine the likelihood of passenger survival based on various factors such as age, gender, class, and more. This project showcases my skills in data preprocessing, feature engineering, model selection, and evaluation. It's a testament to my commitment to harnessing the power of data to solve real-world problems and make informed decisions.

fcc-forum-pageviews.project

For this project, I visualized time series data using a line chart, bar chart, and box plots. I used Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn to visualize a dataset containing the number of page views each day on the freeCodeCamp.org forum from 2016-05-09 to 2019-12-03. The data visualizations helped me understand the patterns in visits and identify yearly and monthly growth.
